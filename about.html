HISTORY

ENCOTECH Ltd Co. has been founded in year 2006 and has started its activities in construction and contracting sectors. ENCOTECH continues its activities as its head office settled in Ankara since 2016. ENCOTECH completed construction projects in the area of substructure, superstructure and industrial type of projects mainly executed in foreigh countries, Ukraine, Georgia, Azerbaijan and Saudi Arabia.

In year 2016, ENCOTECH has settled its head office in Ankara and continues its activities in both local and foreign countries. The founders of company have more than 20 years of experiences and they completed large scale international projetcs executed with foreign EPC companies.

Our primary objective is to ensure full customer satisfaction while serving the specific requirements of clients by providing the best required technology at the globally most competitive prices. We want to create long-term cooperation with our customers .

HEALTH, SAFET AND ENVIRONMENT (HSE)

ENCOTECH is committed to protecting its employees and environment from the effects which may occure during the execution of the project. Success in our business requires planned and systematic identification, elimination and reduction of all risks at all levels. Control of health, safety and environmental risks is our first priority. ENCOTECH shall ensure to comply strictly with related regulations on occupational health and safety, and on the environment, together with the related requirements of related international standards and guidelines as appropriate and applicable for the project.

  • check Creating a healthy work environment and actively promoting the health and well-being of all workers
  • check Establishing plans, procedures of HSE and maintaining good practices in all operations to avoid any probable incidents
  • check Promoting among all workers openness and participation in matters of HSE
  • check Maintaining awareness in HSE by training and awards, in all stages of the project
